Rules:
1. The size of the bot should not be more than 30 x 30 x 30.
2. Maximum of 3 participants are allowed.
3. The bot may be wired or wireless.
4. The power supply will be provided by the college.
5. The bot is allowed to race for 2 times and the best of 2 will be taken into account.
6. If the bot falls from the track for more than 3 times, the bot will be disqualified.
7. Every time a bot falls from the track, 3 seconds will be reduced.
8. The bot that completes the race in minimum time is declared the winner.
9. The decision by the panel is final.
